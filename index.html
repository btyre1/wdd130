Ohio

Ohio is a Midwestern state in the United States that was founded in 1803. It borders Lake Erie to the north, Pennsylvania to the east, West Virginia to the southeast, Kentucky to the southwest, Indiana to the west, and Michigan to the northwest. The capital city of Ohio is Columbus located near the center of the state. It has a population of about 11.8 million and ranks 34th-largest by area.
